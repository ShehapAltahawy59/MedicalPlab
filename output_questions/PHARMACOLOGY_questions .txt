Question 5:
What is the most effective way to treat an overdose of antipsychotics?

A. Activated charcoal
B. Intravenous fluid
C. Intravenous magnesium
D. Intravenous saline
E. Intravenous calcium

Answer: Activated charcoal

Overdose of antipsychotics can be treated by administering activated charcoal. This helps to absorb the remaining drug in the gut, preventing further absorption and reducing the risk of toxicity.
